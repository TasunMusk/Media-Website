ABOUT US

The Richmond College Media Unit, plays a remarkable role in almost all the functions in the college. Though it is still at a tender age, being born in the year of 2001, the victories it has obtained and the services it has rendered are remarkable. It could procreate the Only Presidential Awarded Best Announcer from the Southern Province and also from the origin it could grab many All Island & Provincial victories.

Morning News Broadcast is the lifeblood of the College Media Unit and it is broadcasted in both Sinhala and English languages.

Greatest responsibility of any function organized by/in the school, is shouldered by the announcers of the College Media Unit. It is a great pleasure to mention that the Media Unit is equipped with well talented News Readers, Announcers as well as News Writers and Editors.

Thissa Jayawardana Memorial All Island Announcing Competition which was first held in the years of 2004 & 2008 generated some outstanding Announcers at the All Island level and continues as one of the best media based competitions in the country. The victors of this intense competition are then rewarded for the talent they had shown, at the grand and luxurious awarding ceremony, EAGLE AWARDS.

Moreover, The Rivisara Media Workshop which gave a clear idea about the school media field for the neighbour school announcers is one of the remarkable projects organized by the College Media Unit.

It is the obvious truth that the unchallengeable success of The Richmond College Media Unit is in fact the resultant of dedication, commitment and the support given by the past and the present members of it, for its upliftment.
